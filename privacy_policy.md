**Scope**

The purpose of this APP is to allow you to interact with our Community Exhibition and find out more about the artists who are exhibiting in the exhibition. The APP uses your camera to scan the individual artworks in the Community Exhibition. The App will display profile information about the artist that the artist has uploaded to our secure database themselves and agreed to have their information displayed in the APP for uses to view.
